BACKGROUND OF THE STUDY

Raising awareness through an animation that is inversely related to its actual meaning. November 21, 2019

Reasons why land and sea creatures are dying.

Article 1: "Many animals are dying, scientists believe earth has enetered a sixth mass-extinction." According to Cassie Werber(2015), the decreasing number of land creatures are due to cutting down forests, building cities and using up natural resources can put animals in danger and can even drive them out or their existence.

Article 2: "How a seal pup died with a plastic wrapper in its stomach." According to Bronlow(2015), it shows how a seal pup died due to plastics. He said that its that the plastic piece damaged the tissue of the stomach and allowed bacteria from the gut to flow into the bloodstream. The pup would have died early anyway, but the plastic piece could have sped up the process.

Article 3: "Plastic pollution affects sea life throughout the ocean." According to the united nation(2018), at least 800 species worldwide are affected by marine debris and as much as 80% of that litter is plastic such as wrappers and plastic cups that injested in their stomach causing suffocation, starvation, and drowning.

Usability Test Results

Pre-testing. All of them says that they have an idea why land and sea creatures are dying and all of them says its because of the pollution due to throwing trash everywhere and they are aware that it can affect the environment as well. The possible solution that most of them thought is to stop throwing trash everywhere so that it can lessen the plastics in the environment.

Testing. All of our participants said that our animation is helpful for the environment and it is unique due to the concept.On the other hand, one of our participants said that some people will not fully understand our animation due to lack of knowledge about invesely related topic but they still said that the overall concept of our solution is great.

Post-testing. All of the would apply what they had learned from the animation about of being inversely related. 3 out of 4 of them said that they would recommend it to someone to make people realize the bad effects of them throwing trash in improper places while 1 out of 4 of them would only recommend it to some people because of it being inversely related. All of them likes their experience of watching our animation due to it being inversely related.
